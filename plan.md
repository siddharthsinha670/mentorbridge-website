DESIGN PLAN - MentorBridge Website

PALETTE (from logo):
- Orange: #E85D26 (primary action, logo M-man color)
- Teal: #1D7A6B (Bridge text in logo, trust/education)
- Gold: #C9992A (book/arch in logo, warmth)
- Dark Navy: #0F2137 (deep text, navbar)
- Light BG: #F8FFFE (almost white with teal tint)
- White: #FFFFFF

TYPOGRAPHY:
- Display: "Plus Jakarta Sans" - modern, slightly geometric, friendly yet professional
- Body: "Nunito" - warm, rounded, parent-friendly readability
- Utility: monospace for codes/labels

LOGO COLORS TO USE:
- Orange #E85D26 for CTAs, highlights, icons
- Teal #1D7A6B for trust elements, nav active, headings
- Gold #C9992A for badges, accents, star ratings

SIGNATURE ELEMENT:
The "Bridge" metaphor visualized as a subtle connecting arc/curve between sections 
- The bridge motif appears as a curved divider between hero and content

PAGES (SPA with JS routing):
1. Home - Hero + Why + How It Works + Teachers + Testimonials + Stats + FAQ + CTA
2. About Us - Vision, Mission, Problem, Solution
3. Services - All programs detailed
4. Find a Tutor - Enquiry form
5. Become a Tutor - Registration form
6. Contact - Form + WhatsApp + Map placeholder

LAYOUT:
- Sticky nav with logo + links + dual CTA buttons
- Full-width sections with alternating white/teal-tint backgrounds
- Card-based teacher profiles
- WhatsApp floating button (green)
- Mobile responsive

WHAT'S DIFFERENT FROM GENERIC:
- Logo colors EXACTLY matched (not navy+gold generic edtech)
- Real Hindi/English content from documents
- Bridge curve motif as section dividers
