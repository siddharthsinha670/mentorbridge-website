FAQ

Common Questions

How do I register / find a tutor?
+
Simple! Fill out our "Find a Tutor" form with your child's class, subjects, board, and preferred timing. Our team will call you within a few hours and share 5–10 matching verified teacher profiles. You pick the one you like — completely free.
How are tutors selected and verified?
+
Every teacher goes through three checks: (1) Government ID verification, (2) Educational qualification check — degrees & certificates, and (3) Police background verification. We also conduct a demo evaluation before approving any teacher. Only verified teachers appear on MentorBridge.
Can I take a demo class before committing?
+
Yes — the first demo class is completely FREE. No commitment, no payment required. Your child attends the session, you observe the teacher's style and communication, and only if you're fully satisfied do we proceed. No pressure at all.
How do I pay? What are the fees?
+
Fees vary based on class, subjects, and mode (home/online). After the demo class, our team shares a transparent fee structure with no hidden charges. You can pay monthly via UPI, bank transfer, or cash. No upfront deposit required.
What if I'm not satisfied with the teacher?
+
We have a free Teacher Replacement Policy. Just inform us and we'll arrange a new verified teacher within 48 hours — at no extra cost, no questions asked. Your child's learning never gets disrupted.
Which boards and classes do you cover?
+
We cover Nursery to Class 10 across CBSE, ICSE, and Bihar State Board (BSEB). We have subject experts for Maths, Science, English, Hindi, Social Studies, Computer Science, and more.
How do teachers apply to join MentorBridge?
+
Teachers can apply through our "Apply as Tutor" form. Submit your qualifications, experience, subjects, and documents. Our team reviews within 48 hours and contacts you for the verification and demo evaluation process.
