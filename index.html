FAQ

Questions Parents Ask

How does MentorBridge verify teachers?
+
Every teacher on MentorBridge undergoes three levels of verification: (1) Identity Verification via government ID, (2) Educational Qualification Verification where we check degree certificates and transcripts, and (3) Police Background Verification. Only after clearing all three stages does a teacher appear on our platform.
Is the demo class really free?
+
Yes, the first demo class is completely free — no hidden charges, no commitment required. Your child attends the class, you observe the teacher's style and communication, and only if you're satisfied do we proceed further.
What if we're not satisfied with the assigned teacher?
+
We have a zero-hassle Teacher Replacement Policy. Simply inform us and we'll find a replacement teacher within 48 hours at no extra cost. Your child's learning never gets disrupted, and you don't need to go through the search process again.
Which boards and classes do you support?
+
We cover Nursery to Class 10 across CBSE, ICSE, and Bihar State Board (BSEB). We have subject-expert teachers for all major subjects across all boards.
Do you offer online tuition as well?
+
Yes! MentorBridge offers both Home Tuition (teacher visits your home) and Online Tuition (live interactive video classes). You can choose the mode that works best for your child, or even combine both for maximum flexibility.
What subjects do you cover?
+
We cover all major subjects including Mathematics, Science, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, English, Hindi, Social Science, Computer Science, EVS, and more. We also offer skill programs like Spoken English, Coding, AI Basics, and Personality Development as add-ons.
How do I track my child's progress?
+
Every month you receive a Detailed Progress Report covering subject-wise performance, homework completion rate, attendance, monthly test results, and teacher observations. Every 3–4 months you also receive an Interest Development Report highlighting your child's emerging strengths and suggested activities.

About MentorBridge

Changing How Bihar Learns

We started with one belief — every child in every corner of Bihar deserves a quality, verified, caring teacher. Not just a tutor.

🎯 Our Vision

To become Bihar's most trusted, technology-driven home learning platform — where every parent knows their child's education is in safe, verified, caring hands. We envision a future where quality education is not a privilege of Tier-1 cities but the right of every child in every district of Bihar.

🚀 Our Mission

To connect students with the right verified teachers through a structured, transparent process — and ensure continuous progress monitoring, personalised learning, and holistic skill development so every child reaches their true potential. Not just in exams, but in life.

The Problem We Solve

Why MentorBridge Exists

In Muzaffarpur and across Bihar, finding a trustworthy home tutor has always been a painful process for parents.

⚠️ The Real Problem

  • Parents hire tutors without any background verification or qualification checks
  • Local tutor consultancies only focus on commission — no accountability after placement
  • No tracking of whether the teacher is actually teaching effectively
  • Children stuck in endless trial-and-error cycles for months
  • No progress reports, no parent updates, no monitoring of learning quality
  • Traditional methods create academic pressure without improving understanding
  • Child's confidence and interest in learning gradually decreases

✅ The MentorBridge Solution

  • Triple-verified teachers — ID, educational qualification, and police background
  • 5–10 curated teacher profiles so parents choose the best fit for their child
  • Personalised Learning Plan based on each child's unique learning style
  • Monthly Academic Progress Reports with subject-wise performance data
  • Quarterly Interest Development Report to identify hidden talents
  • Free teacher replacement within 48 hours — no questions asked
  • Future-ready skills: Spoken English, AI, Coding, Personality Development

Our Process

How We Verify Every Teacher

🪪

Identity Verification

Government-issued ID proof (Aadhaar, PAN) collected and verified for every teacher before onboarding.

🎓

Qualification Verification

Degree certificates, mark sheets, and B.Ed / D.El.Ed credentials physically verified by our team.

🛡️

Police Background Check

Police verification certificate required for all teachers to ensure complete safety for children and families.

🎯

Demo Evaluation

Each teacher gives a sample teaching session evaluated by our academic team before final approval.

📞

Reference Check

Previous teaching references and parent feedback checked where available before approving the teacher.

📊

Ongoing Performance Tracking

Even after joining, teacher performance is tracked via parent feedback and monthly progress reports.
