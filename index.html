FAQ

Common Questions

How do I register / find a tutor?
+
Fill our "Find a Tutor" form with your child's class, subjects, board and timing. Our team calls within a few hours and shares 5–10 matching verified teacher profiles. You pick your favourite — completely free, no commitment.
How are tutors selected and verified?
+
Every teacher goes through 3 checks: (1) Government ID verification, (2) Educational qualification check — degrees & certificates physically verified, and (3) Police background verification. We also conduct a demo evaluation. Only verified teachers appear on MentorBridge.
Can I take a demo class before committing?
+
Yes — the first demo class is completely FREE. No commitment, no payment needed. Your child attends the session, you observe the teacher, and only if you're fully satisfied do you proceed. Zero pressure.
How do I pay? What are the fees?
+
Fees vary by class, subject, and mode (home/online). After the demo class, our team shares a transparent fee structure with no hidden charges. Pay monthly via UPI, bank transfer, or cash. No upfront deposit required.
What if I am not satisfied with the teacher?
+
We have a free Teacher Replacement Policy. Just inform us and we'll arrange a new verified teacher within 48 hours — no extra cost, no questions asked. Your child's learning never gets disrupted.
Which boards and classes do you cover?
+
We cover Nursery to Class 10 across CBSE, ICSE, and Bihar State Board (BSEB). Subject experts for Maths, Science, English, Hindi, Social Studies, Computer Science, and more.
How do teachers apply to join MentorBridge?
+
Teachers apply via the "Apply as Tutor" form. Submit qualifications, experience, subjects, and documents. Our team reviews within 48 hours and contacts for verification and demo evaluation.
